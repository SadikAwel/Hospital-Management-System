Our Services

Zewditu Hospital offers a comprehensive range of medical services with state-of-the-art facilities and experienced healthcare professionals.

General Medicine

Comprehensive care for adults including preventive care, diagnosis and treatment of chronic and acute illnesses.

24/7 Emergency Care

Round-the-clock emergency medical services with rapid response teams and advanced life support.

Specialized Care

Expert care in cardiology, neurology, orthopedics, pediatrics, and other specialized fields.

Surgery

State-of-the-art surgical facilities with experienced surgeons specializing in various procedures.

Diagnostic Services

Advanced laboratory and imaging services including X-ray, MRI, CT scan, and ultrasound.

Maternity Care

Comprehensive maternity services including prenatal care, delivery, and postnatal support.
